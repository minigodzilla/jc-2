EASY CONNECTIONS, INTRICATE CONNECTIVITY.

Whichever mode of transportation you prefer, no matter where you have to go, you can be sure easy connections are right around the corner from 1 Jarvis. Highway 403 and QEW lead you to Toronto and the rest of the GTA, while the Lincoln M. Alexander and Red Hill Valley Parkways offer easy travels within the city.

There is also an abundance of GO Stations all within a short walk from home, including Aldershot, West Harbour and Hamilton Stations. The HSR bus stop is only a two-minute walk away and Sobi bikes can be found nestled in pockets around the community.
